How to use Antilapse:

To add an event: Enter a date and time using the menu, and press "Set Coundown". The list will automatically sort based on the nearest date.
To delete an event: Enter the label of the event you wish to get rid of, and press "Remove Countdown".

Antilapse will send you notifications whenever your countdowns reach 1 day, and then 1 hour. Text will change color based on urgency.
It will also generate a quote each time you open the window to remind you that time is relentless, and only you have the power to act before it arrives.
